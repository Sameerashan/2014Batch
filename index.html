Our Legacy

Brotherhood

More than classmates, we became brothers. The bonds we built within the walls of D.S. Senanayake College continue to grow stronger across time and distance. United by shared memories and values, we stand by one another in every stage of life.

Excellence

The 2014 Batch strives for excellence in every field technology, business, medicine, engineering, public service, and beyond. Inspired by our school motto “Country Before Self,” we aim to lead with integrity, leadership, and purpose.

Global Reach

From Colombo to every corner of the world, our batch has created a global network of Senanayake brothers. No matter where life takes us, the spirit of 2014 remains connected, supportive, and proud.
